TIKVAH Token! TKVH


The Tikvah token serves to support the Jacob's Well Farm project.
It was launched without any funding or investors, and we, the founders and participants in the project, purchase the Tikvah by creating a liquidity pool
to ensure the value of the token.
The Tikvah can be used to purchase farm products with special discounts, loyalty rewards, and product customizations.
It can also be used to stay at the Bed and Breakfast.
The Tikvah will initially be available for purchase on the Uniswap platform and will then be expanded to other platforms,
which will be updated on the website https://jacobswellfarm.it/
The progress of the Jacob's Well Farm project will correspond to an increase in Tikvah purchases, thereby increasing the scarcity
of the Tikvah and consequently raising its price.
The Jacob's Well Farm project will use all real-world economy strategies to grow and increase the Tikvah token, one of them being integrating
product production on the blockchain to provide traceability and transparency in the supply chain, allowing customers to know exactly where and
how the products were grown.
